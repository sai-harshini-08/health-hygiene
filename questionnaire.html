HYGIENE

Hygiene refers to the practices and conditions that promote health and prevent the spread of diseases. It encompasses personal hygiene, which includes habits such as regular handwashing, bathing, oral care, and grooming. These practices help reduce the risk of infections and illnesses by eliminating harmful pathogens from the body. Environmental hygiene, on the other hand, focuses on maintaining cleanliness in our surroundings, such as homes, workplaces, and public spaces, through regular cleaning and sanitation. Proper food hygiene is crucial as well, involving safe food handling, cooking, and storage to prevent foodborne illnesses. In healthcare settings, hygiene practices like sterilization and the use of personal protective equipment are vital to protect both patients and healthcare workers. Effective hygiene education promotes awareness of disease prevention methods, empowering individuals
